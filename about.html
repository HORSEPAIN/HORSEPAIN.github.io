WHO WE ARE

OEAA is a consortium of the leading minds in oil and gas. Representing serveral corporations across 7 different nations, OEAA is an intellectual powerhouse in shaping public policy and educational programming. Since 2004 OEAA has been at the forefront of promoting oil and gas education, activism, and investment. We accomplish this through 3 interlinking domains; Lobbying, Education, and Philanthropy.

Education

Our programs cater to all ages, from young children to adults. Our programs are available as drop in sessions or class workshops. For more information please refer to Programs

  • Kids for crude camp (5-12)
  • Oil and Gas day (all ages)
  • Fracking Fridays (13-20)

Activism

We are prominently involved in activism across political and energy sectors, striving to speak for the interests of consumers in an era where policy is drafted not for the benefits of those alive currently, but those who could possible exist in the future.

  • Freedom Convoy logistical support (2022)
  • Model oil pumps donated to Kids' Toy Bank (2020-2024)
  • Orphan well reactivation project (2010-Ongoing)

Philanthropy

OEAA is an ardent supporter of the Capitalist model of altruism, to this end we redirect funds at will to organizations that represent the best interests of today's consumers.

  • Small-scale petrochemical investment
  • Equal access to perflurocarbons
  • Solar farm air rights acquisitions
